### 1. **Monochrome Discipline**

**Masterful**: Linear, Vercel, Stripe

### 2. **Typography Hierarchy**

**Masterful**: 
- **One font family** (Geist Sans, Inter, System)
- **Clear size scale** (12px, 14px, 16px, 20px, 24px, 32px)
- **Consistent weights** (400 body, 600 headers)
- **Tight tracking** (-0.01em to -0.025em)

**Not Masterful**:
- Multiple fonts fighting for attention
- Inconsistent sizes
- Loose tracking (looks amateur)

**HIVE Alignment**: âœ… Geist Sans everywhere, clear hierarchy

### 3. **Spacing Rhythm**

**Masterful**:
- **4px or 8px grid** (everything aligns)
- **Consistent padding** (12px, 16px, 24px)
- **Generous whitespace** (breathes, doesn't feel cramped)
- **Visual rhythm** (cards, lists, sections feel balanced)

**Not Masterful**:
- Random spacing (13px, 17px, 23px)
- Cramped layouts
- No visual rhythm

**HIVE Alignment**: âœ… 4px grid system, consistent spacing scale

### 4. **Border Radius**

**Masterful**:
- **Consistent radius scale** (6px, 10px, 14px, 22px)
- **Heavier than default** (14px+ for cards, not 8px)
- **Feels modern, not harsh**

**Not Masterful**:
- Default 8px everywhere (looks generic)
- Inconsistent radii
- Too sharp (0px) or too round (9999px)

**HIVE Alignment**: âœ… Heavy radius design (14px cards, 22px modals)
