## The Upstream Problem

Students don't know what's happening.

Events exist but they miss them. Spaces are active but they don't see it. Friends are doing things but they don't know.

Campus life is fragmented across 10 apps, 50 GroupMes, and scattered Instagram stories. There's no single answer to: "What's going on?"

**Feed is the answer to "what's happening?"**

## What Makes It Interesting

Interest = **Relevance + Novelty + Stakes**

### Relevance
"This is about my world."

- My spaces doing things
- My friends going places
- Events I'd actually attend

Generic campus news = boring. My corner of campus = interesting.

### Novelty
"I didn't know that."

- New event I hadn't heard of
- New creation that's clever
- New space matching my interests

Same stuff every day = boring. New discoveries = interesting.

### Stakes
"This matters."

- Event happening SOON (urgency)
- Limited spots (scarcity)
- Friends going (social proof)
- My creation being used (investment)

Nothing at stake = boring. Something at stake = interesting.

## The Feed Layers

### The "Right Now" Layer
What's happening RIGHT NOW.

- Events starting in next few hours
- Spaces active right now
- Creations getting responses now
- Friends who are active now

**Why interesting:** Immediacy creates relevance.

### The "Coming Up" Layer
What's on the horizon.

- Events this week you might like
- Trending RSVPs (momentum visible)
- Countdowns to things you're going to

**Why interesting:** Anticipation is pleasurable.

### The "Your World" Layer
What's happening in your spaces and with your people.

- Activity from your spaces
- What friends are doing
- Your creations getting used
- Your events getting RSVPs

**Why interesting:** It's YOUR world. You're invested.

### The "Discover" Layer
Things outside your world you'd probably like.

- Spaces similar to yours
- Events attended by people like you
- Trending creations
- People you might know

**Why interesting:** Genuine discovery.

## Content Mix

### Events (Primary)
Inherently interesting: time-bound, social, actionable, anticipatory.

**Card shows:** What, when (countdown), who's going (faces), quick RSVP.

### Creations (Secondary)
What people are making and using.

**Card shows:** What it does, who made it, usage count, quick use.

### Space Activity (Tertiary)
Signs of life from communities.

**Card shows:** What it is, activity indicator, friends who are members.

### Social Activity (Ambient)
What your people are doing. Woven throughout, not primary content.

"Sarah RSVPed to Photography Walk"
"Jake created a new poll"
