## Post-AI Market Reality

The rules changed. Here's what's true now:

### What AI Commoditized

- **Generic creation is free.** Anyone can generate content, images, basic apps. Creation tools are abundant.
- **Basic personalization is table stakes.** "Recommended for you" isn't a feature — it's expected.
- **Standard support scales to zero.** AI handles 90% of queries.
- **Templates are infinite.** AI generates templates on demand. A library isn't a moat.

### What AI Made Valuable

- **Context is the new scarcity.** Generic AI is everywhere. AI that knows YOUR campus, community, and history is rare and defensible.
- **Network intelligence compounds.** Every interaction makes the system smarter. This data is proprietary.
- **Trust is harder to fake.** Verified .edu emails, real org affiliations, real event attendance — authentic in ways AI-generated communities aren't.
- **Workflow embedding is sticky.** Tools you use once are replaceable. Workflows you depend on daily are infrastructure.

### New Value Pools

Value accrues to:
1. **Proprietary context** — Data and understanding that can't be replicated
2. **Network effects + AI** — Intelligence that gets smarter with more users
3. **Outcome delivery** — Results, not features
4. **Trust infrastructure** — Verified identity, authentic community, real relationships

## Cost Structure

### AI Generation Costs

Current (Groq/Ollama):
- ~$0.0001-0.001 per generation
- Rate limited: 100/hour/user
- Fallback chain ensures reliability

At scale (10K users, 5 generations/user/week):
- 50K generations/week
- $50-500/week
- Manageable

**Key insight:** AI costs drop 10x every 18 months. What costs $500 today costs $50 next year.

### The Ratio That Matters

**Value delivered : AI cost**

| Action | AI Cost | Value Created | Ratio |
|--------|---------|---------------|-------|
| Tool creation (100 uses) | $0.01 | Significant utility | Very favorable |
| Event recommendation (+20 attendees) | <$0.01 | Real engagement | Extremely favorable |
| Smart suggestion (saves 30 min) | <$0.01 | Time value | Extremely favorable |

AI costs are noise compared to value delivered.
