# HIVE Value Propositions

## North Star
50 weekly active creators within 2 weeks of UB launch.

## Primary Value Prop (Leaders)
**"I can make something useful for my people in under a minute and get it in front of them instantly."**

- JTBD: Org leader needs to reach their 60 members with something functional (poll, RSVP, signup), not just a post
- Current alternatives: GroupMe (messages get buried), Google Forms (always broken), Instagram (posts get 12 likes)
- HIVE's answer: Type what you need → get a working app in seconds → place it in your space → members get a push notification
- Win condition: Leader says "this is faster and more effective than my current setup"

## Secondary Value Prop (Members)
**"I can find what's happening and participate in one tap."**

- JTBD: Student wants to know what's going on, RSVP to something, vote on something — without downloading another app or checking another group chat
- Current alternatives: Checking 4 GroupMe chats, scrolling Instagram stories, asking a friend
- HIVE's answer: Open the app → see events happening now → vote on a poll in your space → RSVP to Saturday's event
- Win condition: Student opens HIVE before texting the group chat

## Tertiary Value Prop (Growth)
**"My friends who aren't on HIVE can still use the things I make."**

- JTBD: Creator shares a poll link in their GroupMe → friends vote without signing up → see "Made with HIVE" → some download it
- Mechanism: `/t/{toolId}` standalone URLs work without auth
- Win condition: 20%+ of new signups come from standalone app links

## What HIVE is NOT
- Not a social network (no feed of posts, no likes, no followers)
- Not a messaging app (chat exists inside spaces, not as the product)
- Not a content platform (the content IS the apps students build)
- Not an admin tool (institutional use is a later wedge, not the launch play)

## Value Mechanisms (for feature evaluation)

| Mechanism | How it works | Measured by |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| **Reach** | Leader's creation reaches members via placement + push notification | Notification tap rate, app-to-engagement ratio |
| **Engagement** | Members interact with apps (vote, RSVP, submit) | Interactions per app, unique users per app |
| **Speed** | Time from "I have an idea" to "it's live" | Time-to-first-creation, classification latency |
| **Aliveness** | Surfaces feel active even at low density | Presence indicators, time-based feed content, "Since you left" |
| **Loop closure** | One action leads naturally to the next | % of created apps that get placed, % of placed apps with >0 interactions |
